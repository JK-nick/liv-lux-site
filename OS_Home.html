BOOKING REQUIREMENTS!!

  • Hair must be washed/detangled the day before or the day of your appointment.
  • Make sure hair is free of any oils
  • For less pain or tension to hair, come with hair fully blow dried

Deposits

There is a $20 non-refundable deposit that you will pay once you completely book your appointment

Appointments

  • Please make sure to double-check your name, email and phone number, in case we need to contact you.
  • Book the correct style, time, date and add the proper add-ons. Styles cannot be changed once booked.
  • If you are unsure about length of hair, contact me and send a picture for clarification
  • Please do not book hair appointment with other appointments. Have a clear schedule when booking.
  • If you are unable to make it to your appointment, please contact us 24 hours in advance
  • There will be no late fees but after 30 minutes of lateness, your appointment will be cancelled
  • Travel to client fee will incur a $5 per mile roundtrip fee!!
