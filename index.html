Booking Requirements

  • Hair must be washed, detangled, and fully blow dried the day before or day of your appointment.
  • Hair must be free of oils, creams, or heavy products.
  • Please book the correct style, size, and add-ons. Styles cannot be changed the day of service.
  • Do not schedule other appointments around your braid appointment — timing varies per client.
  • If unsure about hair length or pack amount, please send a photo before booking.
  • If you need help choosing a style, feel free to message me.
💰

Deposit

A non-refundable $20 deposit is required to secure all appointments.

Deposits go toward your total balance and can be sent via Cash App, Zelle, or Apple Pay.

Your remaining balance is due the day of your appointment.

Deposits must be sent within 48 hours after appointment confirmation.

💳

Payment

Full payment is due on the day of your appointment.

Accepted methods: Cash, Zelle, Apple Pay

No refunds once a service has started.

✕

Cancellation

A 24-hour notice is required to cancel or reschedule.

Deposits are non-refundable.

No-call / no-shows will be banned from future booking.

⏰

Late Arrivals

Please communicate if you are running late.

After 15 minutes, a $15 late fee will be applied.

After 20 minutes of NO communication, your appointment will need to be rescheduled.
